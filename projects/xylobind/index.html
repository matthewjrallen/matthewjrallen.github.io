01 / Project

XyloBIND

Help setting up new Wordpress website including content generation and SEO.

02 / Content

The XyloBIND website was established as a professional digital platform to showcase sustainable, lignin-based bio-binders for the UK infrastructure sector. The setup focused on creating a clean, industry-standard interface that effectively communicates technical innovation to contractors and local authorities.

The site’s structure was designed to guide users through key product data, field trials, and sustainability benefits. By mapping content to specific industry needs, the platform provides a clear path from technical education to direct enquiries.

Foundational SEO was integrated to improve visibility for green construction and low-carbon asphalt solutions. This strategic layout ensures the website targets relevant professional searches, positioning XyloBIND as a leader in bitumen replacement technology.
