01 / Project

Happy Cakes

Responsive website built for a local cake shop in Oxford. It features a MYSQL articles section and new online Ecwid shop.

02 / Content

The Happy Cakes website is a bespoke digital platform designed to provide a seamless e-commerce experience for one of the most celebrated bakeries in Oxford. The development focused on creating a high-performance, responsive interface that translates the artisanal quality of the brand into a digital space. By implementing a clean and intuitive user journey, the site ensures that customers can easily navigate through various product categories, including gourmet cupcakes, brownies, and celebratory big cakes.

A primary technical objective was the integration of a robust online ordering system that simplifies the complex logistics of bespoke baking. This included building secure payment gateways and clear call-to-action pathways that allow users to buy online or request custom quotes. The architecture was engineered to handle high-resolution imagery without compromising on page load speeds, ensuring that the visually driven nature of the cake industry is supported by a fast and efficient backend.

From an SEO perspective, the website is structured to dominate local search results for bakery services in Oxfordshire. Each page has been developed with semantic HTML and optimized metadata to target key terms such as "Oxford cupcakes" and "artisan cake delivery." The inclusion of a dedicated articles section and social media integration provides fresh content opportunities, which helps maintain high search engine rankings and drives organic traffic from both local and international customers.

The development also prioritised sustainability and community, mirroring the core values of Happy Cakes. Special attention was paid to the site’s accessibility and mobile optimization, ensuring that the community-focused manifesto is reachable on any device. By combining modern web standards with a customer-centric design, the site acts as a powerful digital storefront that supports the business in its mission to deliver thousands of hand-crafted treats across the region.
